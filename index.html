Armenia

Armenia is a landlocked country in the South Caucasus region of Eurasia,bordered by Turkey to the west, Azerbaijan to the east, Georgia to the north, and Iran to the south. Yerevan, the capital and largest city, is located in the western part of the country. Armenia has a rich history, dating back to ancient times, and it is considered one of the world's oldest countries, with its roots going back to the Kingdom of Urartu (9th century BC).
